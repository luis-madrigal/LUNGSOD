What is the Livability Index?

The Livability Index is LUNGSOD's tool for measuring a city's livability to give you an idea of the quality of life you can attain in a city by giving it a livability score.

What parts of livability does the index measure?

When you think about it, livability is the experience of a combination of factors. That’s LUNGSOD’s Livability Index gives you a livability score based on the total average of eight categories that are considered necessary for livability.

Employment

Housing

Local Government

Transportation

Infrastructure

Recreation
